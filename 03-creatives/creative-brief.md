# Creative Brief

Direction for BodyCheck's pitch creatives, built on `../02-research/brand-kit.md`. The look is one teal heartbeat line on a near-black field: high-tech, calm, premium, lots of negative space.

> Not a medical device. Routing, not diagnosis.

## Brand anchors
- **Palette:** base `#0D1117`, surface `#13181F`, brand teal `#00E5C3`, white text, Action-Needed red `#F87171`.
- **Type:** Space Grotesk for headlines and the wordmark (Fotor substitute: Sora / Montserrat); mono for small labels.
- **Motif:** glowing teal ECG waveform; heart-pulse icon as the logo mark.
- **Workflow:** generate backgrounds in Fotor at the listed ratio, then take to the canvas to add text and the logo from the brand kit. Hex codes only nudge the generator, so lock the true teal on the canvas.

## Rules
- No text, numbers, or letters baked into generated backgrounds; all copy goes on in the canvas.
- Keep the disclaimer line on any asset that makes a product claim.
- Per-state colours (green to amber to orange to red to teal) are a creative device, not yet a product palette; do not present them as in-product.
