Category line, land it once: **Wearables give scores. BodyCheck gives routing.**

**0:25 CORE IDEA** "BodyCheck is an early-warning and care-routing layer..." → one state, one reason, one next step. Not a diagnosis, not a score. Say it: **"We simulate live streams so you see a full week in three minutes."**

**2:35 CLOSE** Students \= less guessing. Universities \= **earlier routing across a cohort, before crisis.** Built: frontend, rules engine, API layer, simulated datasets. Next: clinical review \+ uni wellbeing pilot. End: **"Not a medical device. Silent signals into an earlier route to help."**
